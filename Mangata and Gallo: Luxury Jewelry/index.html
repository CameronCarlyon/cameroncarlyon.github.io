Who are Mangata and Gallo?

Well-regarded for our high-quality and classic look, Mangata and Gallo is a jewelry store that specializes in special occasions such as engagements, weddings and anniversaries. Visit our storefront in Austin, Texas or shop online.

Meet the Owner!

Mariana, an Austin local, is well known for her jewelry designs and is the owner and the lead designer of Mangata and Gallo. After graduating from design school with a specialization in diamond cutting and metal smithing, Mariana opened a store in her hometown, Austin, Texas.
